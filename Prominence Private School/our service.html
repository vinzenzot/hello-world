about us

With integrity and excellence as our focus and goal, at Prominence Private School we strive to provide the best possible education for our students. From humble beginnings of small groups to a fully accredited school, we will continue to strive to provide the best environment we can for every student in our care. Within this beautiful and safe environment, our children are free to experience learning through play and discovery. Teachers make full use of their indoor, as well as outdoor, natural classroom, guiding the children in their social, physical, intellectual, spiritual, creative and emotional development.

Nursery Room

The baby room is for babies between 3 month and 1 year of age. The children’s schedule in the baby class will be prepared to meet their individual needs.

Parents communicate with the teachers the child’s sleeping and eating schedule. And in turn the teachers communicate with the parents daily regarding the child’s naps, meals and diaper changes. Each child has their own space with their own cribs for naptime.

The children do share bouncy seats, swings and highchairs, which are sanitized on a regular basis.

Our baby room teachers offer lots of love to the children and help them discover the world around them as they grow and learn to crawl and walk. In the baby room our teachers work with the children on hand eye coordination an how to entertain themselves through play. The baby room offers both a calm, comforting environment for naptime and stimulating environment for playtime and learning.

We play music and have lots of colorful different toys for the children as they begin to crawl and explore. Our main focus in the baby room is providing the children with lots of love. ADMISSION.

Pre-Primary level

Our Pre- Primary phase is from the age of 2 years to 6 years of age. It is during these ages that we lay the foundation for years to come.

Our daily schedule is filled with various activities such theme discussion, creative art time, fine and gross motor activities, music rings, educational games, free play etc.

Our environment encourages the development of each learner helping and guiding them along the way. A Kindergarten graduation is held at the end of every year, which is certainly one of PPS’s cutest events!

Primary School

Prominence Private School is a registered Private school with the Ministry of Education.

Currently we are accommodating kids from Grade 1 to Grade 3. We are using the Namibian curriculum and extra materials to educate the learner.

Our subjects that we will be focusing on is Mathematics, English, Afrikaans, Environmental Studies, Religious Education, Physical Education, Art, and in addition to core subject students are taught Music.

Throughout the year out trips will be arranged to help the kids learn about the outside world.

Formal homework for grade 1 to 3 is generally encouraged to improve child’s performance.
